Cycling for Sustainability

Freecycling! is a community initiative encouraging people to give away items that they no longer need for free. Our mission is to prevent waste and overconsumption by moving the items that we already have around the community. Not only does it stop perfectly good items from going into landfills, it also means you might get something you're after for free! It is a completely free platform and sign up is super simple. We welcome everyone!
